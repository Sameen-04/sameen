Popular Indian Dishes

Butter Chicken

Creamy, spicy North Indian dish made with marinated chicken in tomato gravy.

Dosa

South Indian crispy rice crepe, usually served with sambar and chutney.

Biryani

Aromatic rice dish layered with marinated meat, spices, and herbs.

Indian Thali

A complete meal with a variety of curries, breads, rice, and dessert on one plate.

Samosa

Fried or baked pastry with a savory potato and pea filling, served with chutneys.
